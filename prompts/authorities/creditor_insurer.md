# Creditor / Insurer

Banks, lenders, and insurance companies that impose conditions on borrowers and policyholders through loan covenants, policy terms, and financial agreements. Entry is voluntary (you took the loan or bought the policy), and exit costs are low -- you can refinance, pay off the debt, or switch providers. Scope is narrow, limited to financial behavior and covered assets.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Low |
| Entry | Voluntary |
| Scope | Narrow |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Mortgage requirement to maintain homeowner's insurance at a specified level
- Auto loan prohibition on modifying the vehicle without lender consent
- Insurance policy exclusion voiding coverage if the property is used for commercial purposes
