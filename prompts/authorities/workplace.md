# Workplace

Employers exercising authority over employees through workplace policies, employment contracts, and management directives. Entry is voluntary (you accepted the job), but exit costs are high because finding new employment takes time and losing income is costly. Scope is medium, covering conduct during work hours and sometimes extending to off-duty behavior.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | High |
| Entry | Voluntary |
| Scope | Medium |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Non-compete clause restricting employment with competitors after leaving
- Social media policy prohibiting public criticism of the company
- Mandatory return-to-office requirement overriding previous remote work arrangements
