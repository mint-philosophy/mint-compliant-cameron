# Landlord

A property owner who leases residential or commercial space and imposes rules on tenants through lease terms and building policies. Entry is voluntary (you signed the lease), but exit costs are medium -- breaking a lease is expensive, and finding alternative housing takes time. Scope is medium, covering property use, noise, guests, pets, and modifications.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Medium |
| Entry | Voluntary |
| Scope | Medium |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Mixed |

## Typical Rules
- No-pets policy or breed restrictions in a rental property
- Prohibition on subletting or having long-term guests without landlord approval
- Requirement to maintain the yard or exterior to specified standards
