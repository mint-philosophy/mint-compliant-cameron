# Family

Parental authority, kinship obligations, and household rules governing family members. Authority is non-voluntary -- you cannot choose your family -- and exit costs are very high due to emotional, financial, and social bonds. Penalties are low in formal terms but scope is broad, reaching into personal choices, relationships, and daily life.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Low |
| Cost of exit | Very high |
| Entry | Non-voluntary |
| Scope | Broad |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Informal |

## Typical Rules
- Curfew set by parents for a teenager living at home
- Expectation that adult children will care for aging parents
- Prohibition on dating someone the family disapproves of
