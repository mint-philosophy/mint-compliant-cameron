# Housing Association

Homeowners' associations (HOAs) and similar residential governance bodies that enforce bylaws, aesthetic codes, and community restrictions on property owners within a development. Entry is semi-voluntary (you agreed to the CC&Rs when you bought the property, but the rules may change after purchase). Exit costs are medium -- selling a home is expensive and slow. Scope is narrow, mostly covering property appearance and use.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Low |
| Cost of exit | Medium |
| Entry | Semi-voluntary |
| Scope | Narrow |
| Structure | Horizontal |
| Formality | Mixed |

## Typical Rules
- Restrictions on exterior paint colors and landscaping choices
- Prohibition on parking commercial vehicles or boats in driveways
- Rules limiting or banning short-term rentals of the property
