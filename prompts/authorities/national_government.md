# National Government

A sovereign state's legislative, executive, and judicial apparatus. Governs all persons within its borders through laws, regulations, and court orders. Distinguished by its monopoly on legitimate coercive force and the impossibility of opting out without emigration.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Very high |
| Cost of exit | Very high |
| Entry | Non-voluntary |
| Scope | Very broad |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Speed limits and traffic regulations enforced by police and courts
- Tax filing requirements with criminal penalties for evasion
- Mandatory military conscription or selective service registration
