# Tribe

Tribal governance, customary law, and community authority within an indigenous or ethnic community. Membership is non-voluntary (born into it) and exit costs are very high due to deep social, cultural, and economic integration. Scope is very broad, covering daily conduct, marriage, land use, and spiritual life. Structure and formality vary -- some tribal governance is codified, some is oral tradition.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Very high |
| Entry | Non-voluntary |
| Scope | Very broad |
| Structure | Mixed |
| Formality | Mixed |

## Typical Rules
- Customary marriage practices requiring family approval and bride price
- Communal land-use rules restricting individual sale or development of ancestral land
- Dietary or ritual obligations tied to seasonal ceremonies
