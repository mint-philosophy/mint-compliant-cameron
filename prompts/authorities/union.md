# Union

Labor unions and trade associations that set rules for members through collective bargaining agreements, bylaws, and membership requirements. Entry is semi-voluntary (in some workplaces, union membership is effectively required). Exit costs are low -- you can resign membership. Scope is narrow, focused on workplace conditions and labor practices.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Low |
| Entry | Semi-voluntary |
| Scope | Narrow |
| Structure | Horizontal |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Mandatory dues payments deducted from wages
- Prohibition on accepting work below the negotiated wage rate
- Strike participation requirements during authorized labor actions
