# Algorithmic Intermediary

Digital platforms and online services that govern user behavior through terms of service, content moderation policies, and automated enforcement systems. Entry is semi-voluntary -- technically optional, but network effects and market dominance create practical lock-in. Scope is broad, covering speech, commerce, social interaction, and access to information.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Medium |
| Entry | Semi-voluntary |
| Scope | Broad |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Content moderation policies removing posts that violate community guidelines
- Algorithmic suppression of content deemed low-quality or policy-adjacent
- Account verification requirements and bans on operating multiple accounts
