# Profession

Licensing boards, bar associations, medical boards, and professional councils that regulate entry into and conduct within a profession. Membership is voluntary (you chose the career), but exit costs are high because leaving means abandoning years of training and livelihood. Scope is narrow -- limited to professional conduct -- but penalties are severe, including loss of license.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | High |
| Cost of exit | High |
| Entry | Voluntary |
| Scope | Narrow |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Mandatory continuing education hours to maintain licensure
- Prohibition on dual relationships between therapist and client
- Reporting requirements for suspected child abuse by medical professionals
