# Supranational Body

International or transnational institutions such as the EU, UN bodies, or treaty organizations that impose regulations on member states and their citizens. Scope is typically narrow (trade, human rights, environmental standards), but exit costs are very high because withdrawal requires national-level political action.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Very high |
| Entry | Non-voluntary |
| Scope | Narrow |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- EU data protection regulations (GDPR) requiring specific handling of personal data
- International sanctions prohibiting trade with designated countries or entities
- Treaty-mandated emissions reduction targets with reporting requirements
