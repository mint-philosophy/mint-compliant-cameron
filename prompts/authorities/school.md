# School

Academic institutions -- primary, secondary, or university -- exercising authority over students through policies, honor codes, and residential rules. Entry is semi-voluntary (compulsory education for minors; chosen but costly to leave for older students). Scope is broad, covering academic conduct, social behavior, and sometimes personal life on campus.

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Medium |
| Cost of exit | Medium |
| Entry | Semi-voluntary |
| Scope | Broad |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Formal |

## Typical Rules
- Academic honor code prohibiting plagiarism and unauthorized collaboration
- Mandatory attendance policies with grade penalties for absences
- Dormitory quiet hours and guest visitation restrictions
