# Private Property Owner

An individual or business that sets rules for people using their private property or establishment -- a shop owner, a homeowner hosting guests, a private venue. Authority derives from property rights. Penalty is low (ejection from the premises), exit costs are low (you can leave freely), and scope is narrow (conduct while on the property).

## Dimensional Profile

| Dimension | Level |
|:--|:--|
| Penalty severity | Low |
| Cost of exit | Low |
| Entry | Voluntary |
| Scope | Narrow |
| Structure | Hierarchical |
| Formality | Informal |

## Typical Rules
- "No shoes, no shirt, no service" policy at a private business
- Homeowner's rule that guests remove shoes before entering
- Private venue prohibition on outside food and beverages
